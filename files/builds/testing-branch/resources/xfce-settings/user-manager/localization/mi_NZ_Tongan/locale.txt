Ngaahi <big><b>kulupu Configuring</b></big>
ʻI he konga ko ʻeni, te ke lava ʻo sio pe ko e fe ʻa e ngaahi kulupu ʻoku kau ki ai hoʻo tokotaha ngaue kuo fili, pea te ke lava foki ʻo liliu ʻa e memipasipi ʻo e kulupu.
Kae tokanga! ʻOku fie maʻu ke ke ʻiloʻi ʻoku totonu ke ke ʻiloʻi lelei ʻa e meʻa ʻokú ke faí. ʻE lava ʻe he vaʻinga holo mo hono ʻahiʻahiʻi ʻo e tokotaha ngaue mo e ngaahi fakangofua fakakulupu ʻo ʻomi hoʻo polokalama kotoa ke taʻofi!
Kapau ʻoku ke fie faʻu pe tamateʻi ha tokotaha ngaue ki he polokalama, ko e meʻa pe ʻoku fie maʻu ke ke fai ko ha toe tokotaha ngaue pe siʻi ange.
Neongo ia, kapau te ke foaki ki ha tokotaha ngaue ha ngaahi totonu lahi ange pe fakangatangata ʻa e ngaahi totonu ʻa ha ʻakauni System pe kulupu ʻo e ngaahi System, mahalo te ke fokotuʻu ʻa e maluʻi hoʻo polokalama ʻi ha tuʻunga fakatuʻutamaki!
Kuo lava lelei hono seivi ʻo e fokotuʻutuʻu ʻo e ʻakauni ngaue!
Kuo pau ke ʻoua naʻa tatau e lea fufū motuʻa ʻo e ʻakauní mo e lea fufū foʻoú!
Kātaki ʻo fili ha lea fufū kehe.
Kuo pau ke ʻoua naʻa toe siʻi hifo he mataʻitohi ʻe 8 ʻa e lea fufū foʻoú!
Oku ikai faʻa tatau a e lea fufū foʻoú mo e lea fufū foʻou naʻá ke fakahuú!
ʻOkú ke fakapapauʻi ʻokú ke fie toʻo ʻa e tokotaha ngāue ki he polokalamá mei hoʻo polokalamá?
Kapau te ke tali >>YES<< ʻe toʻo ʻa e tokotaha ngaue kuo fili mo e ngaahi fakamatala kotoa pe ʻoku fekauʻaki mo ia mei he polokalama ko ʻeni!

ʻIKAI
E ikai lava o tāmateʻi a e tokotaha ngāue ki he polokalama ko ení koeʻuhí he okú ke hū ki he polokalamá ni mo ia!
Kataki ʻo fili ha tokotaha ngaue kehe kapau ʻoku ke fie hoko atu ʻi hono tamateʻi ʻo e kau fakaʻaongaʻi ʻoku ʻikai toe fie maʻu ʻi he polokalama ko ʻeni.
Kuo lava lelei hono tamateʻi ʻo e ʻakauni ngaue ʻaki e ngaahi fakamatala kotoa ʻoku fekauʻaki mo ia!
